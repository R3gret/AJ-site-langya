About Me

I am currently a third-year student at Batangas State University - TNEU Arasof Campus, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a major in Business Analytics. My educational journey began at Eulogio G. Cerrado Elementary School (2010-2016), continued through Core Science Academy Inc. for Junior High (2016-2020), and STI College Balayan for Senior High (2020-2022).

Coming from a family dedicated to service—my father being a barangay captain in Sabang, Tuy, Batangas, and my mother a nurse working in Saudi Arabia—I have grown up with strong values of community and hard work. As an only child, I have developed strong interpersonal skills, enabling me to easily connect with others and build meaningful relationships.

My passion lies in system analysis, where I enjoy diving deep into complex problems and finding efficient solutions. I am also an avid reader who loves exploring different ideas and perspectives through books. Additionally, I have a knack for cooking, which adds a creative and practical side to my skill set. I am driven to further develop my analytical skills and contribute to the world of technology and business analytics.
